Scaling Agrochemical Sales (B2B + B2C) Through Performance Marketing

Client/Company

Essential Biosciences

Role

Performance Marketing Executive

Website Built

Kisan Sewa Kendra

Objective

To drive measurable growth in both B2B lead generation (bulk orders from agro-dealers, retailers, distributors, and wholesalers) and B2C e-commerce sales (direct-to-farmer purchases via Shopify), leveraging Meta Ads, Google Ads, Shopify store development, and CRM integration.

Part 0: Shopify Store Development

  • Built the Kisan Sewa Kendra Shopify store from scratch for Essential Biosciences.
  • Designed product catalogue structure for fertilisers, pesticides, herbicides, fungicides, and plant growth regulators.
  • Integrated payment gateways, shipping, discount systems, and analytics tracking.
  • Ensured mobile-first UI/UX, catering to farmers browsing primarily on smartphones.
  • Linked Shopify with Google Ads, Meta Ads, and CRM for seamless tracking of orders and leads.

Part 1: B2B Lead Generation

Strategy

  • Designed and executed Meta Ads campaigns segmented by regional languages (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, Malayalam).
  • Focused on conversation-based lead generation to connect prospects directly with sales reps.
  • Implemented CRM with structured leads and deals pipeline, ensuring lead tracking from capture → qualification → closure.

Results

  • Total Leads Generated: 12,000+
  • Cost per Lead (CPL): ₹4.61 – ₹32.03
  • Reach: 2.1M+ prospects
  • Impressions: 6.9M+
  • MQL Conversion Rate: 20–25% during peak season
  • Closed Revenue Impact: ₹45+ Lakhs attributed to lead-driven deals

Part 2: B2C E-Commerce Growth

Strategy

  • Scaled Meta Ads and Google Ads targeting farmers across India.
  • Used audience segmentation by crop and product category, A/B tested ad creatives, and optimised landing pages.
  • Linked Google Ads + Meta Ads data with Shopify for full-funnel visibility.
